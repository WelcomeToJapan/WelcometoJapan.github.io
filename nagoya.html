Welcome to Nagoya

A major economic powerhouse with a rich historical background and a burgeoning tourism scene.

Nagoya, the capital of Aichi Prefecture, is a significant industrial center and the heart of Japan's manufacturing industry, particularly known for its automotive sector and cutting-edge technology. Must-Visit Attractions

Key sights that showcase Nagoya's history, innovation, and beauty.

Nagoya Castle

A magnificent castle with golden shachihoko (tiger-fish) on its roof, a symbol of Nagoya.

Atsuta Jingu Shrine

One of Japan's most important Shinto shrines, housing a sacred sword.

Osu Kanon

A fanous Buddhist temple conveniently close to good shopping, parks, and nightlife.

Experiences in Nagoya

Immerse yourself in Nagoya's unique blend of culture, cuisine, and innovation.

Savor Nagoya Meshi

Indulge in local specialties like Hitsumabushi (eel), Miso Katsu, and Tebasaki (chicken wings).

Automotive Exploration

Visit the Toyota Kaikan Museum or take a factory tour (advance booking required).

Modern City Views

Ascend the Nagoya TV Tower or Midland Square Sky Promenade for panoramic cityscapes.
