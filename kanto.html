Top Destinations in Kanto

Explore the diverse cities and natural wonders of the Kanto region.

Tokyo

The vibrant capital, a blend of tradition and futuristic innovation.

Hakone

Scenic beauty, hot springs, and views of Mount Fuji.

Nikko

UNESCO World Heritage sites and stunning natural landscapes.

Yokohama

A modern port city with a vibrant Chinatown and beautiful bay views.

Enjoy the Hakone Open-Air Museum, cruise Lake Ashi, and soak in an onsen.

Visit Nikko's Shrines

Discover the ornate Toshogu Shrine and the natural beauty of Kegon Falls.

Savor Kanto Cuisine
