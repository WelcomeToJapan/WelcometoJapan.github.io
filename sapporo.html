Sapporo: Hokkaido's Urban Gem

Discover a vibrant city known for its beer, ramen, and the famous Snow Festival.

Welcome to Sapporo

The largest city in Hokkaido, offering a perfect blend of urban excitement and natural beauty.

Sapporo is the bustling capital of Hokkaido, renowned for its lively atmosphere, delicious cuisine, and the iconic Sapporo Snow Festival. Despite its urban sprawl, the city is surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes, making it a gateway to Hokkaido's wilderness.

From exploring its vibrant entertainment districts to enjoying its famous beer and ramen, Sapporo offers a unique urban experience distinct from other major Japanese cities, with a relaxed pace and plenty of green spaces.

Must-Visit Attractions

Iconic landmarks and popular spots in Sapporo.

Odori Park

A beautiful urban park that hosts the famous Sapporo Snow Festival.

Hokkaido Shrine

A significant Shinto shrine located in Maruyama Park.

Susukino

Sapporo's vibrant entertainment and nightlife district.
