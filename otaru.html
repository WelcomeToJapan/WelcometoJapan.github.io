Otaru: Hokkaido's Romantic Canal City

Explore historic canals, glass factories, and delicious seafood.

Welcome to Otaru

A charming port city with a nostalgic atmosphere, just a short trip from Sapporo.

Otaru, located on the west coast of Hokkaido, is renowned for its picturesque canal, historic brick buildings, and vibrant glassworks. Once a major port city, Otaru has beautifully preserved its past, offering visitors a nostalgic journey through its charming streets and alleys.

Beyond its historical appeal, Otaru is a paradise for seafood lovers, particularly famous for its fresh sushi and seafood bowls. The city also boasts a thriving arts and crafts scene, with numerous glass factories, music box museums, and local artisan shops.

Must-Visit Attractions

Key sights that capture Otaru's unique charm.

Otaru Canal

Take a stroll or a boat cruise along the historic canal, especially beautiful at dusk.

Fresh Seafood

Indulge in Otaru's renowned fresh sushi and seafood bowls.

Sakaimachi Street

A bustling shopping street lined with glass shops, cafes, and seafood restaurants.

Experiences in Otaru

Immerse yourself in Otaru's unique culture and activities.

Fresh Seafood & Sushi

Indulge in some of the freshest seafood and sushi Hokkaido has to offer.

Glassware & Crafts

Explore numerous glass shops and even try your hand at glassblowing.

Canal Cruises

Take a leisurely boat ride along the Otaru Canal for unique perspectives.
