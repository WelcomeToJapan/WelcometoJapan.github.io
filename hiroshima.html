Hiroshima: A Message of Peace and Hope

Explore a city that rose from ashes to become a symbol of resilience and peace.

Welcome to Hiroshima

A city with a profound history, now a vibrant hub for peace and remembrance.

Hiroshima holds a significant place in world history as the first city to suffer an atomic bomb attack. Today, it stands as a powerful symbol of peace, resilience, and the human spirit's ability to overcome adversity. The city has been rebuilt with a focus on peace and remembrance, attracting visitors from around the globe.

Beyond its solemn memorials, Hiroshima is a vibrant, modern city with a rich culinary scene, beautiful gardens, and easy access to the stunning Miyajima Island, famous for its "floating" torii gate.
