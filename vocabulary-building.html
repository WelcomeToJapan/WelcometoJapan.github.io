Vocabulary Building for Travelers

Essential words and phrases to navigate your trip to Japan.

Words to Make Your Trip Smoother

Knowing even a small vocabulary can significantly enhance your travel experience.

While many Japanese people in tourist areas may speak some English, having a grasp of basic Japanese vocabulary will not only help you navigate more easily but also show your appreciation for their culture. This page provides a curated list of words and phrases useful for common travel situations.

Practice these words, and don't be afraid to use them! Even small attempts are often met with warmth and encouragement.
