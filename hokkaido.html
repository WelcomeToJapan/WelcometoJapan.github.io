Welcome to Hokkaido

Japan's largest and northernmost prefecture, known for its untouched nature and distinct seasons.

Hokkaido is a land of vast open spaces, dramatic volcanic landscapes, and pristine national parks. Unlike the more densely populated main island of Honshu, Hokkaido offers a refreshing escape into nature, with distinct seasonal beauty from vibrant flower fields in summer to world-class powder snow in winter.

It's also a paradise for food lovers, famous for its fresh seafood, dairy products, ramen, and unique local specialties. Whether you're seeking outdoor adventure, culinary delights, or serene natural beauty, Hokkaido delivers an unforgettable experience.
