Welcome to Hakone

A popular resort town known for its natural beauty and proximity to Mount Fuji.

Hakone is a picturesque town nestled in the Fuji-Hakone-Izu National Park, famous for its hot springs (onsen), natural beauty, and the iconic views of Mount Fuji across Lake Ashi. It's a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of Tokyo, offering a blend of relaxation, art, and outdoor activities.

Visitors can enjoy boat cruises on Lake Ashi, ride the Hakone Ropeway for breathtaking views, explore outdoor art museums, and indulge in traditional Japanese hospitality at a ryokan.

Must-Visit Attractions

Key sights that make Hakone a memorable destination.

Lake Ashi (Ashinoko)

Enjoy scenic pirate ship cruises with views of Mount Fuji.

Hakone Ropeway

Glide over volcanic valleys with stunning views of Mount Fuji and Lake Ashi.

Owakudani

A volcanic valley with active sulfur vents and views of Mount Fuji.
